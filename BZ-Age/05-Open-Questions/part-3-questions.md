## Current state of the 4.2-kiloyear event debate
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** Recent paleoclimate synthesis. The dating and severity of the event have been contested.
- **Why it matters:** The contrarian beat about climate-collapse being oversold depends on the current state of this debate.

## Best treatment of Enheduanna's hymns and their political function
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** Recent translations and scholarly treatments. The "first known author" framing is strong but needs to be grounded in current scholarship.
- **Why it matters:** Enheduanna is the proposed opening hook for Part 3.

## Most current synthesis on Akkadian Empire administration
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** Recent comprehensive treatment. The Akkadian Empire is one of the less-studied early imperial systems.
- **Why it matters:** Multiple structural points depend on this — governors, hostages, language policy.

## Comparison literature on Akkadian vs. Old Kingdom Egyptian governance
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** A solid comparative treatment, or two treatments that can be compared.
- **Why it matters:** Part 3 uses Egypt's Old Kingdom as the parallel imperial case. Need to know how well the comparison holds.
