# Part 2 — Open Research Questions

Open questions specific to *The First Cities and the Invention of the Future*.

## Current state of Uruk-period demographics
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** Recent estimates of Uruk's peak population. The numbers in older syntheses vary wildly.
- **Why it matters:** Scale-translation depends on knowing how big the city actually was.

## How decisive is the temple-state model in current scholarship?
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** Recent treatments of early Mesopotamian political economy. Some scholars push back hard on the temple-state framing.
- **Why it matters:** The "Uruk was not utopian" contrarian beat depends on the extracted-labor framing. If the temple-state model is contested, the beat needs nuance.

## Best treatment of Sumerian women's lives
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** A solid recent synthesis. Especially on elite women (en-priestesses) whose voices we have.
- **Why it matters:** Anonymous female scribes are a Part 2 lesser-known fact. Need evidence base solid.

## Most current synthesis on cuneiform's invention
- **Raised:** 2026-05-26
- **What would close it:** Recent work on the token-to-writing transition. Denise Schmandt-Besserat's framework has been the standard; needs sanity check.
- **Why it matters:** The "writing evolved from accounting" beat is central to Part 2's Rule 9 content.
