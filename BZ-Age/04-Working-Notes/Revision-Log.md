- **Patterns eliminated across all parts:**
  - Preamble-before-doing: "Let me tell you about X", "Let's talk about X", "I want to talk about X" → replaced by just doing it
  - "Here's the thing about X" / "Here is where the story gets..." → said the thing directly
  - Meta-commentary: "This is what we're doing here", "Sit with that" (standalone), "I want you to actually sit with that", "And I want you to hold this in your head" → cut or replaced by writing that earns the weight
  - Announced contrasts: "The standard story goes: X. [Thing] says: no." / "Not X. Not Y. But Z." → contrarian points argued as natural evidence-led argument
  - Announced transitions: "Now: [topic]." / "Back to X." / "Let's turn to X." → transitions made, not named
  - Telling the listener how to feel before creating the experience → instruction cut, experience delivered
  - "I am not going to tell you X. I am going to tell you Y." constructions → "X is not Y. It is also Z."
  - "What I can tell you is X" → just told X
