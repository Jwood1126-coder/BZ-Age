# Memory, Myth, and the Long Echo

**Status:** Active throughline.

## What it is

The Bronze Age ended but did not disappear. It became the *Iliad*, the Hebrew Bible's deep background, the legends of fallen kingdoms barely surviving in scattered later references. Track how the period was remembered, forgotten, mythologized, rediscovered.

## Why it matters

The Bronze Age's afterlife is part of its meaning. Also: the contrast between the civilizations that left foundational texts (Egypt, Mesopotamia) and those that didn't (Hittites, Mycenaeans-as-themselves) is itself a lesson in historical visibility — who gets remembered and why.
