# The Invention of Historical Memory Itself

**Status:** Active throughline.

## What it is

The Bronze Age is where written history begins, but it's also where the deliberate manipulation of historical memory begins. Egyptian pharaohs erasing predecessors' names from monuments. Mesopotamian kings claiming to restore the laws of long-dead predecessors to legitimize themselves. The Sumerian king list extending back into mythical reigns of tens of thousands of years.

The Bronze Age invents both history and the political weaponization of history at the same time.

## Why it matters

Methodologically — teaches the listener how to read past propaganda. Also: this is where the modern political-history machine begins. Every subsequent regime that has rewritten the past for present purposes is operating in a tradition the Bronze Age invented.
