served as high-level story architect for this series, per `04-Working-Notes/fable-master-prompt.md`.
Everything in this folder is designed to outlive the model that wrote it: taste codified into rubrics,
architecture decided and defended, and generators another model can keep running.

**Who reads this.** The writer (Jake), and whatever model holds the pen after Fable — expected to be
Opus 4.8. If you are that model: start with `01-opus-operating-manual.md`, then the decision log.

when a rubric and your instinct conflict, flag it for the writer rather than resolving it silently.

## Standing rules for this folder

- Every factual specific carries `[VERIFY]` unless it is already fleet-verified in
  `04-Working-Notes/draft-04-fleet-findings.md` or the research digest.

  replacement prose. The pen belongs to the narrator's single voice.
- Menu, not verdict: favorites are marked ★ and argued; the writer discards freely.
